Heavy Bag

Heavy bag is a very effective full-body workout equipment.
Punching the bag improves upper body strength and power over time.
The muscles in the arms, shoulders, legs, back, chest & core are all
engaged in a heavy bag training session. Learn more

Boxing Gloves

There are three types of gloves; training, sparring & competitiion gloves.
Training gloves are designed for hitting bags
rather than people while sparring gloves
are used when training with a partner. Learn more

Boxing Hand Pads

Boxing hand pads are used to compress (and keep compressed when hitting)
the bones & tissues in the hands. It can be worn under boxing gloves.
Although it does not have the same impact as gloves it helps keep the fist in proper
shape & support the wrist by keeping it aligned while delivering a punch. Learn more
